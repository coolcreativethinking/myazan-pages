My Azan - Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 22, 2026

Overview

My Azan is an Amazon Alexa skill that provides Islamic prayer times, Azan (call to prayer) playback, Quran recitation, and related Islamic calendar features. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information My Azan collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

Information We Collect

My Azan collects and stores the following information to provide its services:

  • City and Country — Your city name and country, provided when you say “set my city to [city name]”. This is used to calculate accurate prayer times for your location.
  • Geographic Coordinates — Latitude and longitude derived from your city name via the AlAdhan API. These are used for local prayer time calculation.
  • Timezone — Your timezone, determined from your city, used to display prayer times in your local time.
  • User Preferences — Settings you choose, including Azan reciter, playback speed, calculation method, iqama offsets, Taraweeh time, and Quran recitation preferences.
  • Alexa User ID — A hashed (anonymized) version of your Alexa account identifier, used to store your settings across sessions.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name, email address, or phone number
  • Your home address or precise location (only city-level)
  • Voice recordings or conversation history
  • Financial or payment information
  • Health or biometric data

How We Use Your Information

  • Prayer Time Calculation — Your city and coordinates are used to calculate daily prayer times using the adhan.js library locally or via the AlAdhan API.
  • Personalization — Your preferences (reciter, calculation method, etc.) are stored so you don’t have to set them every time you use the skill.
  • Hijri Calendar — Your country is used to provide region-specific Hijri date adjustments for Ramadan, Eid, and other Islamic events.

Data Storage

  • Amazon S3 — Your settings are stored in Amazon’s S3 service via the Alexa Skills Kit persistence adapter. This data is tied to your Alexa user ID and stored securely within Amazon’s infrastructure.
  • Supabase — If you enable optional features (such as automatic Azan), a hashed version of your Alexa user ID and your city/preferences may be synced to our Supabase backend. Your raw Alexa user ID is never stored — only a SHA-256 hash.

Third-Party Services

My Azan uses the following third-party services:

  • AlAdhan API (aladhan.com) — For prayer time calculation and Hijri calendar data. Your city name is sent to this API.
  • Amazon Web Services — For skill hosting, data storage, and the Alexa platform.
  • EveryAyah.com / Islamic Network CDN — For Quran audio recitation files. No personal data is sent to these services.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is only shared with the third-party services listed above as necessary to provide the skill’s functionality.

Data Retention

Your settings are retained as long as you use the skill. If you disable or remove the skill from your Alexa account, your S3-stored data will be automatically deleted by Amazon. You can reset your settings at any time by saying “set my city” to a new location.

Children’s Privacy

My Azan is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Change or update your settings at any time by voice command
  • Remove the skill from your Alexa account to delete stored data
  • Contact us to request deletion of any data stored in our backend

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
