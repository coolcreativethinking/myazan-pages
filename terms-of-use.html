2. Description of Service

My Azan is a free Alexa skill that provides:

3. Prayer Time Accuracy

Prayer times are calculated using established astronomical methods (ISNA, MWL, Egyptian, Umm al-Qura, and others) via the adhan.js library and the AlAdhan API. While we strive for accuracy:

  • Prayer times are approximations based on mathematical calculations and your city-level location.
  • Times may vary slightly from those published by your local mosque or Islamic authority.
  • You should always verify prayer times with your local Islamic center for religious obligations.
  • We are not responsible for any discrepancies between calculated times and official local times.

4. Hijri Calendar

Hijri (Islamic calendar) dates are based on the Umm al-Qura calendar with optional adjustments. Actual moon sighting may differ from calculated dates. For religious observances such as the start and end of Ramadan, always follow guidance from your local Islamic authority.
